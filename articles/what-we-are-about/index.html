What we are about

2018-07-04 | 44 words

Chasing the sun westward
At thirty five thousand feet, and
On the remotest mountain valley
Are the fingerprints of our fellow man

The hard edges of our ordered way
Between cloud and snow
Define and declare who we are, and
What we are about
